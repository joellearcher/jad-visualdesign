Prototype

I created my mockups in Adobe XD. I chose an artboard with the size of 1280 x 800 which is a standard web size for a HD screen.
For the mobile device, I chose 375 x 812 which works for most current mobiles.
I researched travel websites focused on the Caribbean for styling ideas. The majority had a white background with bright coloured navigation or logo.
As there are only two main functions of the website, to inform (list of islands) and play (dice game), I chose to design a website that was bold, uncluttered and minimalist.
